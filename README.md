# GameSub

I use this client side html page to figure out the subs for the basektball team that I coach. If you want to use it, open it directly in your preferred web browser. 

https://trevstephenson.github.io/GameSub/

If you want to adapt it to your own players, just open the source code and look for the array that contains the players names, you can modify them to suit your needs.

Also, this sub planner only does between 6 and 9 players. For 5 players or less there are no subs, then for 10 players just sub 5 on each time, that's really the easiest way to do it. If you have 11 players or more in your squad then I recommend cutting some players to bring it down to 10 or less.
